MockJEE - A mock test platform

MockJEE is a test platform for teachers and students. As a teacher you just focus on curating questions and our platform will seamlessly create tests . Students can take tests on their smartphone or web.

An Android app is available for taking IIT JEE mock test: Participate in India's largest IIT JEE mock test to predict your rank, learn areas of weakness and rehearse for real exam.
